DrinkPal

Project status: in development

My Role: sole REST API server developer, sole Database developer

Product Description
DrinkPal is an android social application for drinkers looking for a drinking buddy. With this app, the user can invite friends or total strangers to come drink with them. Alternatively, the user can instead get notified when other people around his/her area are looking for a drinking buddy and join them. The app also have various additional features, such as the ability to attach photo or video of their lively surrounding as they invite people to join them for a drink.

Project notes
As the sole server developer and database developer, I am in charge of both implementing and maintaining our Loopback server and our MySql database. I am also in charge of implementing and maintaining packages on the client side (the android app) that are in charge of communication between the server. Finally, I setup and maintain our cloud solutions.
