Songify

Project status: on hold

My Role: full stack developer

Product Description
Songify is a dynamic music streaming web application. This web application, allows users to listen to music, create playlists, like musics, follow music artists, customize the page theme, and much more. Furthermore, Songify allow artists to upload their music, and even their album, customizing their artist page with own images, and even see analytical data of their song. Finally, everything is managed by an admin team that has admin tools. These tools grants the staff team to verify artists, verify songs, ban users, receive suspicious activity flags and much more.

Project notes
Even though I worked on both front end and back end, I was more responsible on the back end systems. I was solely maintaining the database, as well as the account system. In addition I was heavily involved in making this web application extremely secure, implementing advance security systems and custom security frameworks. This is my first time programming with PHP, however, at that time, I already had some experience in OOP.
