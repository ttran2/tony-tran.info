Edumundo ProSim Analyzer

Project status: completed

Line of codes: currently approx. 1500 (6 modules)

Description
Edumundo ProSim Analyzer is a desktop CLI application that aids company management in Edumundo's Advanced Management Simulaton game. With this tool, staying ahead of the competition is much easier, as it gives the management team a better overview and insight of their competitors. It can also flag suspicious companies that are most likely managed by your teachers.

Edumundo Management Simulation ProSim Advanced is a strategic management simulation, where students act as Directors of their own company and work in teams, competing against each other, to gain market share (user manual here). Students have to make cruicial operational decisions in various departments such as Marketing, HRM, Purchasing, Finance, etc.

Additional notes
With the help of this tool, I was able to win first place in the competition (and 2x Raspberry Pi 4) in our BIIT (Business in IT) module. In addition, I correctly identified companies controlled by our professor.
